5 Mistakes to Avoid When Packing Your Storage Unit

Oct 24, 2025 Admin 2 Comments

Moving into a storage unit can save you space and stress, but only if done correctly. Avoid these common pitfalls to ensure your belongings stay safe and accessible.

1. Using Old or Weak Boxes

It's tempting to grab free boxes from the grocery store, but they often lack the structural integrity needed for stacking. Invest in sturdy, uniform moving boxes to prevent crushing and to maximize vertical space.

2. Not Labeling Everything

You might think you'll remember what's in "Box 3", but six months from now, you won't. Label every box clearly on multiple sides. Consider creating a master inventory list.

3. Skipping the Climate Control

For sensitive items like electronics, wood furniture, and photos, standard units might expose them to extreme temperatures. Climate control is worth the extra cost for long-term preservation.

Pro Tip: Leave a walkway in the center of your unit so you can access items in the back without unloading everything.
